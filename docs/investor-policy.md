# QUICKMELA INVESTMENT POLICY (DRAFT FOR REVIEW)

**Issued by:** Tekvoro Technologies Pvt Ltd (QuickMela)

**Effective:** [date]

## 1. Purpose
To offer qualified investors the opportunity to invest capital into QuickMela operations in exchange for a contractual return (fixed interest or revenue share) — without offering equity or company ownership.

## 2. Nature of investment
Investments are contractual obligations of QuickMela to pay returns per agreed terms. Investors receive no company shares, voting rights, or ownership.

## 3. Investment products and minimums

**Fixed ROI Plan — Minimum ₹25,000**

- 12 months → 15% return (example)
- 18 months → 18–24% (tiered, based on amount)

**Revenue Share Plan — Minimum ₹50,000**

- 2–5% revenue share on revenue generated using the invested pool until 120–130% total return achieved.

## 4. Payout frequency
Investor may choose Monthly / Quarterly / End-of-tenure. Payouts are processed to a verified bank account after KYC, subject to reconciliation.

## 5. Lock-in & early exit
Lock-in as per plan (e.g., 12 months). Early withdrawal allowed only with penalty (2–5%) and Board approval — subject to liquidity and reconciliation.

## 6. Fees & taxes
Company fees (admin & platform) may apply (1–2%). Taxes (GST, TDS) are the investor’s responsibility where applicable; the company will issue receipts and deduct TDS where required by law.

## 7. Risk disclosure
All investments carry business risk. Past performance is not a guarantee of future returns. Investors should read the Investment Agreement for full terms.

## 8. Legal
Investors must sign the Investment Agreement and undergo KYC. Disputes are resolved via arbitration in Hyderabad. This program is not a public deposit scheme.
