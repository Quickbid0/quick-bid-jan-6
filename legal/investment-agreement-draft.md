**Date:** [date]

## 1. Definitions

1.1 **"Investment Amount"** — sum provided by Investor under this Agreement.

1.2 **"Plan Type"** — Fixed ROI or Revenue Share.

1.3 **"Tenure"** — specified in Schedule A.

1.4 **"Return Target"** — amount the Investor receives as payout (principal + return).

## 2. Nature of relationship

Investor provides funds. This Agreement creates a contractual repayment obligation. Investor is not a shareholder; there is no equity, no voting rights, and no management role.

## 3. Investment, receipt and use

3.1 Investor will transfer the Investment Amount to a designated escrow/collection account.

3.2 Company will use funds for QuickMela growth activities as described in Annex B.

## 4. Payment of returns

4.1 **Fixed ROI:** Company pays **[X%]** of the Investment Amount as return, by **[monthly/quarterly/end]**.

> Example: 15% on ₹100,000 after 12 months = ₹115,000.

4.2 **Revenue Share:** Company pays **[Y%]** of revenue generated by the pool until the Investor receives **[Z%]** total return.

## 5. Security & priority

5.1 Investments are unsecured contractual obligations of the Company. No security over assets is granted unless specified in Annex C.

## 6. Taxes & deductions

6.1 Company shall deduct any withholding tax (TDS) when legally required and provide tax certificates.

## 7. Events of default

7.1 Insolvency, bankruptcy, or prolonged failure to pay triggers default remedies including dispute resolution & recovery.

## 8. Early termination

8.1 Early withdrawal is permitted subject to penalty and the Company’s liquidity at the time of request.

## 9. Representations & warranties

9.1 Investor confirms authority to invest.

9.2 Company confirms valid incorporation and authority to enter into this Agreement.

## 10. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep financial terms confidential, except where disclosure is required by law or regulators.

## 11. Governing law & arbitration

11.1 This Agreement is governed by the laws of India.

11.2 Disputes are resolved via arbitration in Hyderabad under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act.
